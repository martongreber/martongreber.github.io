Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025.07.30.

Marton Greber ("Company", "we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Policy describes how we handle information when you use any of our mobile applications (each, an "App", and together, the "Apps") and any related services (collectively, the "Service").

1. No Third-Party Ads or Trackers

Our Apps do not display third-party advertising and do not integrate third-party analytics SDKs, trackers, or social-media plugins.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No personally identifiable information (e.g., name, email, phone number).
  • No device identifiers (e.g., IDFA, GAID, IMEI) transmitted to our servers.
  • No location data (GPS, Wi-Fi, or Bluetooth beacons).

3. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The Apps may store limited functional data only on your device, such as:

  • Game or activity progress (e.g., levels completed, daily rewards).
  • Local preferences (e.g., language, sound settings).

This data never leaves your device unless you manually back it up via your operating system’s backup service. We do not have access to it.

4. Consumption Data for Refund Processing

Why we share: When you request a refund for an in‑app purchase on the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, those platforms may ask us for proof that the purchased content was used or "consumed." To meet that requirement, we may securely transmit minimal consumption information—such as the timestamp you first accessed a purchase or the percentage of a subscription period already used—to the respective platform.

Scope: We share only the data strictly necessary to help Apple or Google decide the refund request. This data is used solely for that purpose and is handled in accordance with each store’s policies. No other personal or device data is transmitted.

5. Children’s Privacy

The Apps are not directed to children under 16. We knowingly collect no personal data from anyone under that age.

6. Data Security

Because we do not maintain a remote user database, there is no centralized server for an attacker to breach. Any information stored locally is protected by your device’s operating‑system security.

7. Your Choices & Rights

  • You can reset or delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the App or using the in‑App reset option (where available).
  • If future versions introduce cloud features, we will update this Policy and obtain any required consents (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Policy to reflect changes in the Apps or legal requirements. We will post the revised Policy in the Apps or on our website and update the Effective Date above. Continued use of the Service after the update constitutes acceptance.
