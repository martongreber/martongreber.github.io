Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 18, 2026.

Marton Greber ("Company", "we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Policy describes how we handle information when you use our mobile applications (each, an "App", and together, the "Apps") and any related services (collectively, the "Service").

1. No Third-Party Ads or Trackers

Our Apps do not display third-party advertising and do not integrate third-party analytics SDKs, trackers, or social-media plugins.

2. Information We Do Not Collect on Our Own Servers

  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not collect or store your name, email address, phone number, or precise location on our own servers.
  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies in the Apps.

3. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The Apps may store limited functional data locally on your device, such as:

  • App or feature usage state (for example, progress, completed actions, or daily rewards).
  • Local preferences (for example, language or sound settings).
  • Purchase state needed to unlock premium features on your device.

We do not have access to data stored only on your device. However, data stored on your device may be included in backups or other services provided by your operating system or platform provider, subject to their own terms and privacy policies.

4. Purchases, Subscriptions, and Refund Processing

In-app purchases, subscriptions, billing, and refund requests are processed by the applicable platform provider, such as Apple or Google, under their own terms and privacy policies.

Where supported or required for refund-related workflows, we may provide limited purchase-use or consumption information to the applicable platform provider, such as whether purchased content was accessed or approximately how much of a subscription period had been used at the time of the refund request. We share only the minimum information reasonably necessary for that purpose.

We do not use this information for advertising or profiling.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the limited information handled in connection with the Apps. Information stored locally on your device is generally protected by your device’s operating system and security settings. Purchase, billing, and refund-related information handled by Apple or Google is subject to their own security practices and policies.

6. Your Choices & Rights

  • You can stop using the Apps at any time.
  • You can delete locally stored app data by uninstalling the App or using any in-app reset option, where available.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple App Store or Google Play account settings, as applicable.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Policy to reflect changes in the Apps, applicable law, or platform requirements. We will post the revised Policy in the Apps or on our website and update the Effective Date above.
